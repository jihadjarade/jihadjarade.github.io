More Information

When choosing the shade percentage of window tint, it's important to consider both your personal preferences and legal regulations in your area. A window tint shade of around 35-50% is commonly recommended for a balance between style and functionality. This level of tint provides a moderate reduction in glare and heat while still maintaining good visibility during the day. When choosing the shade of window tint, consider your preferences, local regulations, and the level of privacy and protection you desire. Consult with a professional window tinting service to select the right shade that meets your needs and adheres to legal requirements.
